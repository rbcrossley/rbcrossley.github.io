This blog is about my journey of making money online blogging with BerojgarEngineer.com.

I have just right now applied for google adsense and purpleads. I guess most of you have heard about Google Adsense. Purpleads is exactly like Adsense. It is alternative. Both are advertising platforms that pay their publishers.

A little bit of con about Purpleads is that it requires 500 USD for withdrawing in your bank account. Since PayPal does not work in Nepal, I had to use it.

Another thing is that it is Israeli owned company. Just telling in case that matters for you if you factor political and ethical concerns.

The pros is that there is no minimum traffic requirement explicitly written. 

Google Adsense is another global platform for publishers to earn income by selling advertisement spaces in their site.

I have applied to Google Adsense 1 week ago.

I also have an youtube channel and a facebook page. 

As of 2026/05/31 2:42 PM, none of my platforms are monetized.

In this blog I will share about earnings from every platforms that I will receive.

My target is to make $1000 in 1 year combinedly from all of these platforms.


What are my analytics?

Not much. To be really honest. I got lot of traffic from posting in reddit. But that is about it. The organic search traffic is probably myself clicking the link after searching for keyword in google. That is why currently I do not expect to be approved either in Purpleads or google adsense.


Update: I just got rejected from purpleads.
