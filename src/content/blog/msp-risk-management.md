## How MSP® Risk Management Improves Programme Success

Ever wondered why some programmes succeed while others fail despite having similar resources, talent, and goals? The answer often lies in how well **risk** is identified, assessed, and managed throughout the programme lifecycle.

### What is MSP®?

MSP® (Managing Successful Programmes) is a globally recognized framework that provides a structured approach to programme management. One of its strongest features is its emphasis on integrated risk management.

### Key Principles of MSP® Risk Management

- **Continuous Risk Identification** - risks are reviewed at every stage
- **Stakeholder Engagement** - engaging key stakeholders to surface risks early
- **Adaptive Response Planning** - responses adapt as conditions change
- **Integrated Reporting** - a single risk register feeds into all decisions

### Why It Improves Success Rates

Programmes that adopt MSP® risk practices report higher delivery rates, lower cost overruns, and better stakeholder satisfaction.
