## Digital Tools for Modern Freight Dispatchers

The freight industry has undergone a digital revolution. Modern dispatchers no longer rely on paper logs and phone calls—they leverage powerful software platforms to manage fleets in real time.

### Key Takeaways

- Real-time GPS tracking improves route accuracy
- AI-powered ETA prediction reduces customer service calls
- Integrated communication tools cut downtime
- Mobile-first dispatch apps speed up response

### Top Digital Tools

1. **TMS (Transportation Management Systems)** - centralized planning
2. **GPS Telematics** - live vehicle tracking
3. **ELD (Electronic Logging Devices)** - compliance with hours-of-service
4. **Route Optimization AI** - dynamic re-routing
5. **Driver Communication Apps** - replacing radios and phone calls
