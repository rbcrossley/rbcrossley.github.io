## How Pakistani CS Students Can Build a Career Path That Goes Global

The global tech industry is more accessible to Pakistani CS students today than ever before. With the right approach, you can compete with developers from anywhere in the world.

### Step 1: Master the Fundamentals

Deeply understand:

- Data Structures & Algorithms
- Operating Systems
- Networking
- Database concepts

### Step 2: Build a Portfolio

GitHub is your second resume. Build at least 3 substantial projects.

### Step 3: Contribute to Open Source

Contributing to popular open-source projects shows initiative, gets you noticed, and builds your network.

### Step 4: Network Globally

Join Twitter/X, Discord communities, and LinkedIn. Attend virtual meetups and conferences.

### Step 5: Apply Strategically

Apply to remote-friendly companies, attend international hackathons, and consider freelance platforms to start earning in foreign currency.
